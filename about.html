WHO WE ARE

OUR STORY

Hey 2014 ... 1975 called and they want their contracts back. You can almost hear it. Someone looking for a paper contract, stored in a metal filing cabinet somewhere. Untraceable, unsearchable, unconnected to what's happened since it was signed in 1975. Contracts are the legal foundation of doing business with others. They contain key business data that is the foundation for partner and customer relationships. But they are still mostly done on paper and sent for review, editing and signing, even in the face of major shifts in how companies of all sizes do business. Even though CRM, ERP and team software tools in the cloud are being adopted at an increasing pace, contracts have been trapped in the past. Contractually was born out of the frustration of working in such a painful, paper-plenty process while living in a digital world. Contractually can be summed up in the question, "why send a document to a person when you can invite them in?" Words like invite, share, collaborate, visibility, search ... they are the heart of our cloud solution aimed at making contracts dramatically easier and faster to complete. So you can get on to the real task of building and managing your business relationships.
