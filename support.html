SUPPORT
What is Contractually? ▻
Contractually lets you create, share and sign contracts in the cloud. You'll never chase a Word contract by email again.
Is Contractually an e-signature solution? ▻
Contractually is much more than an e-signature solution. We automate the entire contract process ... but e-signatures happen to be included as a feature.
Does Contractually provide contract templates for me? ▻
Yes. Contractually offers a number of basic templates to help you create great-looking contracts. However most of our customers upload their existing Word-based contracts into a simple online version that's easier to share and track.
Can I upload my own templates? ▻
Yes. You can upload your existing contracts into online versions that can be shared, tracked and signed entirely online.
What is a template? How is it different from a contract? ▻
A template is a contract that can be reused over and over again. Turn your existing contracts into simple web forms. Your collaborators will only have to change those elements that are unique to each contract.
Can I upload my PDF templates? ▻
You can copy and paste text from some PDF contracts into a new template in Contractually. If you can select the text in your PDF, you can usually paste it into a contract template.
What if I already use an e-signature solution? ▻
Contractually offers all the benefits of an e-signature solution. It also adds the ability to speed up the creation and sharing of contracts, track changes, collaborate and stay up to date with contract status, all in real-time.

What does it mean to invite someone to a contract? ▻
Once you create your contract in Contractually, you can invite others to view, comment, edit, share and sign - all in a simple, secure and intuitive online interface. When you invite someone into a contract, you can give them certain permissions to review, edit, fill in fields and sign. Instead of creating a contract in a word processor and emailing it to each party, the right way to get contracts signed fast is to create, share, edit and sign them entirely online.
Don't I have to send the other person/party a contract document to sign? ▻
No. You are no longer forced to send an email (or worse, paper) to each party to get their feedback or sign the contract. Everything is done online. Invite your parties to the contract, and get notified when they have edited or signed your contract.
Can I sign before I've invited someone? ▻
No. Contractually works just like any other contract process. Share it with your other parties, ensure all content is acceptable, and then each party can sign online.
Can the other party see my edits and comments? ▻
Any comments and edits you make before sharing are not visible to other parties. Once the contract has been shared, all edits and comments are visible to each party. You can exchange private messages about the contract with other members of your organization, and these are not visible to other people.
How do I add users in my company? ▻
From the menu beside your avatar, select Organization, then select +Member to invite other members of your team. This will send them a secure invitation to sign up and join your organization's account. You will be able to add users up to the maximum allowed for your plan. For one-off contracts, you can invite people in your company using "Invite" in the application. It is important that invitees DO NOT forward the link to contracts to other people.
What if the other party doesn't want to use an e-signature? ▻
We know what it's like to have old-school friends and colleagues. Once your contract has been shared, any party can select Paper Options just to the lower right of the contract. Once they print it out, sign it and scan it, and send it back to you, it can be uploaded in PDF format from the same menu. You can mark the contract as Complete there too.
What are fields? ▻
Fields are blocks of text that you can change for each contract. Let's say you have a Sales Agreement where you know the customer name, address, effective date, price and jurisdiction changes for every contract that's created. Each of these blocks can be created as fields. Rather than hunting through a document to change the text for each contract, simply fill in a form with all the field data on it. It will be automatically populated into the contract.
How do I set up fields? ▻
Create a template. Click the edit button to edit the template. Highlight to select the text you want to turn into a field. With it highlighted, select the green "+Field" button just to the right of the template, and enter all the default information for the field (a field name, default text, and a short description). As soon as you create a contract from this template, every field you've created will be presented for you to enter in one place in the fields wizard.
How does pricing work with Contractually? ▻
Contractually's pricing is based on feature sets, on a per user per month basis. A user is someone who initiates, reviews, views, edits, fills out fields, signs or accesses contracts in Contractually in your organization. This does not extend to individuals in other parties you are doing the contract with.
