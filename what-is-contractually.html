What is Contractually?
Contractually helps people work better together, both inside and outside your organization. Why send a contract out when you can invite someone in? Sharing is where it’s at.

SUPPORTS WORKFLOWS

Turn on features to handle complex review and approval workflows, and make sure all parties have status updates. Your team can instantly see contract activity so they can promptly act on a contract when it’s their turn.

Using our data gateway (API, Application Programming Interface) or Salesforce.com connector, data flows easily into and out of contracts to other systems.

In an interconnected world, with today’s cloud­based software and open data standards, integrating new technologies to solve old problems is easier than ever.

Spend less time chasing emails

Spend your time sealing the deal instead of trying to figure out who received what version of the contract, and when. Invite people to work on the contract online. They can be people inside your organization, or outside. You choose. Keep track of who’s been invited, and who’s responded.
