FEATURES

COLLABORATION

Contractually helps people work better together, both inside and outside your organization. Why send a contract out when you can invite someone in? Sharing is where it’s at.

Spend less time chasing emails

Spend your time sealing the deal instead of trying to figure out who received what version of the contract, and when. Share contracts digitally, with no need to email, print or open in other software. Contractually helps you see the progress of each contract instantly, and receive up-to-the-minute status updates. Invite people to work on the contract online. They can be people inside your organization, or outside. You choose. Keep track of who’s been invited, and who’s responded.

Work together, better, faster

Upload your existing contracts to get started quickly. Or use Contractually’s design templates to create clean, clear, easy-to-read documents. Not every document goes through the same review. Contractually helps you set permissions for who can create, edit, review and sign contracts. Share messages privately with your colleagues when you need to, or message everyone. Share comments on the text, and track the history of changes made to each contract. Contractually’s easy-to-read, easy-to-use layouts make it simple to collaborate.

Make things easier for everyone

Attach any schedules and files to the contract as you’re working on it. The attachments stay with the contract and become part of the signed agreement. E-signatures make signing fast and easy, with levels of security and detail that go beyond ink. Still want a PDF? No problem. Export completed contracts to PDF and send it to everyone in one click.

mport information directly from any CRM system, including Salesforce, to customize and generate a contract. Export information directly from Contractually to trigger post-contract processes like invoices. Contractually’s API seamlessly integrates with your CRM and other applications, including billing and corporate document management systems, to speed up workflows.

CONTRACT MANAGEMENT

Stay organized

Keep contracts stored securely in one place. Find what you need quickly with Contractually’s advanced filter and search function. No more hunting around in various folders and drives.

Get more control

Control who's invited to collaborate on the contract with permissions and custom workflows. Contractually’s tracking and audit feature lets you see every change made, reducing the risk of edits slipping through unnoticed.

Stay Secure

Get the added security of tracking who signed what and exactly when. Contractually’s encrypted e­signatures lock down the contract with any attachments you’ve added, together with a traceable record of edits and reviews. Contractually’s encrypted e­signatures give you added assurance on identity and security. More reliable than ink, e­signatures are a faster, easier way to get your contracts completed.

Sign online securely

Invite someone to sign with their email address, and get your contract signed with a legal, encrypted electronic signature. Gain confidence in knowing that the identity of your signers is assured, and the document they signed is permanently recorded.

Create contract templates

Make the process of creating contracts simpler by creating contract templates that you can use over and over again. Change only the information that's unique, like the names of parties, signatories, dates, price and product selection, service offerings and terms. Set your templates up so that any trusted colleague can re­use them, and create contracts in minutes.

Import existing contracts

Your contracts and templates are all stored online in digital form with Contractually. Get started quickly by uploading your existing contracts in MS­Word format, or cut and paste from other sources. Format your contracts so that they look great online and are easy to review, edit and sign.

Add attachments

Upload important attachments and schedules to the contracts you share with other parties. They're permanently associated and bundled with the contract as it moves through the approval process. When your signatories apply their e­signature to the contract, they're signing for all the attachments as well.

Set contract permissions

You maintain control over who can view, edit and sign each of the contracts that you share. Contractually helps you protect confidentiality, so the right parties have access to the right contracts at the right time. Manage the workflow Create a Contractually account with a record of all the people in your organization who create and share contracts. Invite your colleagues who need to manage permissions, create or edit contracts, sign them, or simply review them. Contractually will keep your colleagues up to date on the latest status of each contract they have access to.

Send and receive messages

Share messages with colleagues in your organization, or with everyone on the contract. Selectively share comments and revisions with key parties to the contract.

Audit and track contract status

Keep track of who's seen the contract, who's edited it and when. Contractually sends you notifications when important events occur, like when invitees have opened the contract, made a change or even signed it. Auto­reminders go out when parties haven't taken a requested action, to help keep the process on track. Compare versions Get your whole contracts team on the same page. Easily track contract changes from version to version without uploading or emailing Word documents. Contractually provides true cloud functionality for your documents with full­featured editing and change tracking.

Keep your contracts secure

Contractually servers are hosted by one of the most trusted and reliable providers in the industry, with 99.99% uptime. Our platform uses 256­bit SSL encryption to store and back up your data and contracts securely.

Filter and search

Find what you’re looking for quickly. Tag your contracts to make them easier to find. Filter contracts by party, date, status and tags you create. Contracts are stored securely in an online repository for you and your colleagues to retrieve at any time.

Integrate with your existing systems

Generate contracts for review and signing directly from your CRM with one click. The information from your CRM auto­populates the contract template you choose. Auto­email the contract for signature in minutes, and invite team members to join the review and editing collaboration.
